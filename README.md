# 📊 Bank Marketing Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a bank marketing dataset to understand customer behavior and identify factors influencing term deposit subscriptions.

The project includes:

- Data Cleaning
- Feature Engineering
- Exploratory Data Analysis
- Data Visualization
- Business Insights
- Interactive Power BI Dashboard

---

## Business Problem

The bank wants to improve the success rate of its marketing campaigns by understanding which customers are more likely to subscribe to a term deposit.

## Key Insights

- Customers aged 31–50 have the highest subscription rate.
- Higher account balances are associated with higher subscriptions.
- Longer call durations increase the likelihood of subscription.
- Management and technician occupations contribute significantly.
- Previous successful campaigns improve future conversions.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing on high-conversion customer segments.
- Optimize campaign timing based on monthly trends.
- Improve engagement with customers having high balances.
- Prioritize follow-up for customers from successful previous campaigns.
